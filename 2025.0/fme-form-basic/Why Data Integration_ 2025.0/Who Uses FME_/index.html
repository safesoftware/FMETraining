Which Industries Use FME?

Various industries use FME due to its flexibility and ease of use. Not only are 500+ formats supported, but community members also upload custom content to FME Hub, which is free to access.

    Airports & Aviation
Vancouver International Airport (YVR) used FME as the data integration backbone for its enterprise digital twin, translating GIS, LiDAR, and infrastructure data into interoperable formats and real-time APIs. This enabled live visibility into assets, maintenance activities, and passenger flows while eliminating data silos across airport systems. By operationalizing spatial data at scale, YVR streamlined airport operations and improved passenger experience, setting a new benchmark for data-driven airport management.

     Architecture, Engineering, and Construction
MGN Civil automated its 24/7 drilling QA/QC workflow by using FME to ingest Trimble GPS XML data, validate compliance, and publish near–real-time results to ArcGIS Online dashboards for field crews. The FME-driven pipeline eliminated manual Excel processing that consumed two staff up to six hours per day, reducing turnaround from days to minutes and allowing non-compliant holes to be identified and corrected while the rig was still on site. This avoided costly re-mobilization of equipment and significantly reduced operational risk on a high-volume marine ground improvement project.
